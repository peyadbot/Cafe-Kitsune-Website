Experience the exceptional flavors of Burundi's Yandaro Washing Station coffee. Built in 1986, this station processes coffee from over 3,000 smallholder farmers. With meticulous attention to detail, the cherries undergo rigorous sorting, fermentation, washing, and drying. The result is a clean, complex, and flavorful coffee. Brew it with a 1:16.5 ratio, filtered water at 206-208℉, medium grind, and 3-3.5 minute brew time. Discover the remarkable taste of Yandaro Washing Station's Burundi coffee today.
